Instructions

  • Player 1: Picks any batch of stones on their side. Drop one stone in each hole in a counter-clockwise motion. If the player encounters their winning bucket, drop one stone in. If the player encounters their opponent's bucket, ignore and continue dropping stones
  • Free Turn: If the last stone is dropped in players bucket.
  • Capture: if the last piece dropped in was in an empty hole. The player grabs the stones in the opponents hole.
  • End Game: When all six spaces on the board are cleared. The player who still has pieces on his side of the board when game ends gets all of those pieces
  • Winner: Player with the most stones in his/her bucket.
